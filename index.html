ABOUT DIREWOLF

DireWolf Kernel was started by initially by LordShenron for Xioami Redmi 5A(Riva) and it focused mostly on making the sound capabilities of that device better. Later it evolved into a full Customized Kernel with a motive to increase the overall performance of the device. Team Wolf was formed when Enzaik and Kamil joined and started working to make Direwolf better. Now we are increasing our support for many device slowly.
